Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website or by phone, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property address or location details
  • Photos of trees or property you share with us for quoting purposes
  • Details about the tree service you're requesting

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. We do not collect information automatically beyond what is standard for web hosting (such as server access logs).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Provide quotes and estimates for tree services
  • Schedule and coordinate service appointments
  • Follow up on completed work

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Formspree — Our contact form submissions are processed through Formspree. When you submit the contact form, your information is transmitted through their service to reach us. You can review Formspree's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.
  • Google Fonts — We use Google Fonts to display text on our website. Google may collect basic usage data through this service.
  • Google Maps — Our contact page may include an embedded Google Map. Google's own privacy policy applies to their map service.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tracking, or any advertising scripts on this website. We do not track your browsing behavior. Standard web server logs maintained by our hosting provider may record basic access information such as IP addresses and pages visited, which is standard for all websites.

Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as it is needed for legitimate business purposes, such as providing a quote, completing a service, or maintaining records of work performed. If you'd like us to delete your information, just ask and we'll take care of it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your personal information
