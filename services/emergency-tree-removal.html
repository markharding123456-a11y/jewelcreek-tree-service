Emergency Tree Removal

24/7 rapid response when disaster strikes

When You Cannot Wait, Neither Do We

A tree through your roof at 2 AM. A massive cottonwood blocking the only road to your property. A snapped trunk leaning on a power line with live wires sparking in the rain. These situations do not happen on a convenient schedule, and they cannot wait until Monday morning.

Jewel Creek Tree Service provides 24/7 emergency tree removal across the Boundary region. When you call, we mobilize. Our crew carries the equipment, training, and experience to handle the most urgent and dangerous situations safely, and we prioritize getting you out of harm's way as fast as possible.

Emergencies We Respond To

  • Trees fallen on homes, garages, and outbuildings
  • Trees blocking roads, driveways, and access routes
  • Storm-damaged trees threatening to fall
  • Trees on or near downed power lines
  • Root-failure trees leaning on structures
  • Wind-split trunks at risk of complete failure
  • Post-wildfire hazard tree assessment and removal
  • Heavy snow load damage and breakage

Our Emergency Response Process

When you call our emergency line, we gather the key details: location, what the tree has hit or is threatening, whether anyone is in danger, and whether power lines are involved. If live wires are down, we will coordinate with BC Hydro before any work begins. Safety comes first, always.

On arrival, our crew secures the scene, stabilizes any partially failed trees to prevent further damage, and then begins systematic removal. We carry chainsaws, rigging gear, and lighting for night operations. For trees on structures, we use careful sectional cutting to remove weight without causing additional damage to the building.

After the Emergency

Once the immediate threat is resolved, we can help with full cleanup, stump removal, and an assessment of any remaining trees on your property that may have been weakened by the same storm. We also provide documentation that many insurance companies require for damage claims.

The best emergency is one that never happens. Consider our tree trimming services and proactive danger tree removal to reduce the risk of emergency situations. Visit our FAQ page for more information about emergency response times and what to expect.
